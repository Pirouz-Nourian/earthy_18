This folder contains 1 Rhino file and 1 grasshopper script.

- The Rhino file contains 1 surface and 5 points.

- The GH file draws approximate thrust lines from points on a surface depending on the curvatures along the surface.

Outputs:
- A vector of the thrust direction at the input point.
- Thrust line.
- Length of each thrust line.

Plugins required: GHPython.

The GH-definitions have instructions included.
